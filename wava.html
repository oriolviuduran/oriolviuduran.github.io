About
WAVA is a short cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer about self-growth and finding your place. It is my first solo project and I'm currently improving some aspects of the full-game prototype that I developed as my End of Degree Project. I am also working on adding new mechanics and functionalities that will enhance the overall game experience. These improvements include adding sound, music, the ability to push and pull boxes, a new UI, and more.

Accolades
  • My End of Degree Project, which consisted in creating a prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks.

Inspiration

  • Game Art: Familia (Sebastian Curi, 2016)
  • Game Sound: Gymnopédie No. 1 (Erik Satie, 1888)

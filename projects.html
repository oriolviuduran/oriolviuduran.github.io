Recreational Electronic Works

Team Project (RecElec) | Master's Thesis (Uppsala Universitet)

TBD3 Unity> 4 months

itch.io | Trailer

  • Role:

    Creative Director / Technical Game Designer / Programmer / Sound Designer

  • Learnings:

    Counterfactualism and satire for social commentary, in-game economy balancing, immersive sound and user experience, designing a unique concept and setting.

  • Summary:

    Business simulator revolving around the what-if scenario of an early version of video games - known as RecElecs - being introduced in Victorian era London. Gameplay based on directing the RecElec production, managing the workforce, and trying to keep a sinking factory afloat.

About Me
A little backstory
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I was born in Girona in 1999. For as long as I can remember, I have always loved creating stories, characters and worlds. As soon as I saw the chance of turning this into a career, I knew I had to take it.

Current doings
I have recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games at the University of Girona (UdG). My End of Degree Project was awarded full marks and consisted in designing and developing WAVA, a cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer about finding your place.
I am currently looking forward to joining a game studio as a designer and, on my free time, work on solo projects so as to keep learning, growing and improving my skills as a game developer.

Me, according to myself
I consider myself to be a responsible, logical and creative person with a high capacity for team-working and very keen to keep learning. My main hobbies are playing video games, running, collecting vinyls and anything geography-related.

About Me
Personally
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I'm a Game and Narrative Designer. I would describe myself as creative, analytical, detail-oriented and an easily adaptable team player. Also a cozy gamer, amateur runner, vinyl collector and a bit of a geography buff!

Professionally
I'm currently looking to join a studio around Barcelona or abroad where I can learn and grow both on a professional and personal level. I develop small narrative-driven games in solitary under the name North Oriole with the intention of expressing myself and improving my skills. WAVA, a cinematic puzzle-platformer about self-growth and finding your place, has been my most notable released project so far. I also enjoy participating in game jams and other team building activities. Very Mush Alike, our entry for the Indie Spain Jam 2023, was chosen as one of the 25 finalists, ranking 21st overall and 13th for its game design and mechanics.

Academically
On 2022 I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games from the Universitat de Girona. My Final Degree Project, which consisted in designing and developing the prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks and the "Computer Field Award" at the 28th Edition of the Premis Patronat. A year later I attended the course in Narrative Design for Video Games from Pulse College to improve my writing and interactive storytelling skills.

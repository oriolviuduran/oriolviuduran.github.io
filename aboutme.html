About Me
Personally
Creative, analytical, detail-oriented and easily adaptable. Able to communicate, collaborate and work efficiently in teams, as well as having the initiative and versatility to work independently when required. Passionate about running, record collecting, geography, Sherlock Holmes, The Simpsons and story-driven video games. According to Bartle's player taxonomy, somewhere between explorer and achiever!

Professionally
Currently looking for a game design role that allows me to prove myself, learn and grow both on a professional and personal level. In addition, developing small narrative games in solitary under the name North Oriole with the intention of expressing myself and improving my skills. Most notably released WAVA, a cinematic puzzle-platformer about self-growth and finding your place. Also interested in participating in game jams and other group activities, with some previous projects being positively received and accoladed.

Academically
Graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games from the Universitat de Girona. My Final Degree Project, which consisted in designing and developing the prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks and the "Computer Field Award" at the 28th Edition of the Premis Patronat. A year later attended the course in Narrative Design for Video Games from Pulse College to improve my writing and interactive storytelling skills.

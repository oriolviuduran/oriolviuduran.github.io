About Me
A little backstory
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I was born in Girona in 1999. For as long as I can remember, I have always loved creating stories, characters and worlds. As soon as I saw the chance of turning this into a career, I knew I had to take it.

Current doings
In 2017, I enroled in the Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games at the University of Girona, my hometown. I am currently working on my end of degree project, which consists in creating a prototype for a cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer game, called WAVA. Afterwards, I plan on finishing WAVA, keep working on other solo projects and join a team as a Game Designer. Alternatively, I would love to go abroad for my Master's degree.

Me, according to myself
I consider myself to be a responsible, well-organized and creative person. Some of my hobbies are video games (duh), basketball, running, music, cinema, photography and geography.

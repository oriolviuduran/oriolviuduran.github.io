From Girona With Love.
About
This project began in 2020, stemming from my post-lockdown walks through the empty streets of Girona. Everything had changed; my hometown looked lonely. However, at the same time, it had never stood out more to me. I started taking lots of pictures on my phone - trying to capture this new-found solitude - and posting them on Instagram under the name From Girona With Love. .

Colour and composition are two of the main aspects I tend to use to create this narrative through my pictures. Every new post is always accompanied by a song recommendation, therefore combining two of my own interests: music and visual arts.

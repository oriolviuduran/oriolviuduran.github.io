About Me
A little backstory
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I'm a Game Designer and Developer. I was born in Girona in 1999 and, for as long as I can remember, I have always had a passion for crafting stories, characters and worlds. I chose this career because it allows me to merge my creative and imaginative side with the analytical and technological one.

Current doings
On 2022 I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games from the Universitat de Girona. My Final Degree Project, which consisted in designing and developing the prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks and the "Computer Field Award" at the 28th Edition of the Premis Patronat.

I'm currently looking to join a game studio and begin my professional career. I also work on narrative-driven solo projects under the name North Oriole Games with the intention of expressing myself, learning and growing.

Self-portrait
I consider myself to be an organised, communicative, versatile and creative person, with a high capacity for critical thinking, teamwork and always very eager to keep learning and growing. My main hobbies include playing video games, running, collecting vinyls and learning anything and everything geography-related.

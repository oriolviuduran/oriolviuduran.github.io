Role & Responsibilities
  • Writer & Narrative Designer
    The word 'evig', which means 'eternal' in Swedish, was used as an acronym for the central poem about the prevalence of nature over humanity. Each verse begins with each letter in the word 'evig', and it goes as follows: "Even after humanity fails, Visions from the past stood infernal; In need of anything that prevails, Glad we know nature is eternal."

  • Game Designer & Programmer
    The core game loop revolves around QTEs, as the player presses each letter when the bird character overlaps with the portal-like object. Every time the right letter is pressed on time, a new verse of the poem is shown, and the background gets progressively engulfed in greenery and nature.

Facts & Accolades
  • Entry for the My First Game Jam 2024, organised by the Game Design Department at Campus Gotland of Uppsala University and targeted to its new students. The main theme for the Jam was "Invincibility" - as well as the two bonus challenges "Impressionism" and "Narrated" - and all projects had to be made in Scratch.

  • Duration: Eight hours

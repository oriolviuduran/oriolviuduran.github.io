Detailed Analysis
  • Narrative Design:
    The main purpose of WAVA is to transmit a message of self-discovery using only visual storytelling. The story is told mainly through the game loop and the main mechanics, as well as the relationship and dynamic between the two main characters, each one being the carrier of a different narrative theme. Visual aspects like the weather help enhance the story, along with other level elements such as bridges or caves.

  • Level & Systems Design:
    Although the game is intended to be linear and easy to play, it still feels engaging by combining elements of combat, logic, puzzles and different types of movement. The game dynamic changes from chapter to chapter so as to keep the game refreshing and to mirror the story. Finally, a collectible system evokes a sense of exploration to more experienced players and gives the game more replayability.

  • Art & Animation:
    As the game itself, the art and visual aspect is very minimalist, conceptual and atmospheric. Nonetheless, thanks to its distinct vector art-style and approximately 35 different spritesheet animations, the game has a very unique appearance.

  • Sound & Music:
    For the auditory part of the game I worked with a good friend of mine who is a musician. We worked together along several in-person sessions to create a sound that complements the game's feel. It is the only aspect that has not been made exclusively by myself.

About
In 2020, as soon as I began wandering through the empty streets of Girona after being at home for so long, everything changed. I saw my hometown differently; it was lonely, but it had never stood out more to me. I then started taking pictures of it on my phone and posting them on Instagram under the name From Girona With Love.

I try to focus on aspects such as colour or composition, as I have always been interested in cinematography and other visual arts. To me, nothing would be complete without music so, for each post, I always recommend a song.

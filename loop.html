Details & Analysis
  • Gameplay & Narrative Design:
    The main purpose of LOOP is to soothe and calm the player with simple relaxing gameplay. The core game loop revolves around a looping Circle that must dodge black triangles and, as higher scores are reached, new colours are unlocked. The circular movement of the character is an allegory to how the Circle feels, anxious and trapped inside its mind. On the other hand, the visual simplicity and nothingness of the world represents how empty the Circle sees the world. An existential question is asked at the beginning of every game and, when it is over, a positive and hopeful response is given.

  • UI & UX Design:
    In order to keep the interface as minimalist and clean as possible, there are no buttons and interaction is based solely on simple touch input. Very few colours are used so as to make the customisable detail colour stand out as much as possible, which can be seen on the Circle and a few UI elements. The game does not include any ads or microtransactions in order to reinforce its positive message and make progress only possible through the player's perosnal growth and improvement.

  • Music & Sound Design:
    The auditory aspect of the game is intended to be relaxing and ethereal, so as to stay in line with the main narrative themes and the simplicity of the visual style.

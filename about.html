About Me
A little backstory
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I was born in Girona in 1999. For as long as I can remember, I have always loved creating stories, characters and worlds. As soon as I saw the chance of turning this into a career, I knew I had to take it. In 2017, I enroled in the Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games at the University of Girona (UdG), my hometown.

Current doings
I have recently finished my End of Degree Project, which was awarded full marks and consisted in designing and developing WAVA, a cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer about finding your place. I am currently looking forward to joining a game studio and keep working on my solo projects. Alternatively, I would love to go abroad for my Master's degree.

Me, according to myself
I consider myself to be a responsible, well-organised and creative person. Some of my hobbies are video games (duh!), basketball, running, music, cinema, photography and geography.

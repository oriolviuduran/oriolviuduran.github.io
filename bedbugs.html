Role & Responsibilities
  • Sound & UX Designer
    Due to the wondrous nature of the story told, bringing the imaginative scenarios to life was a must. This was achieved by changing the music and soundscape for each scene, designing a fun and high-pitched sound for the speech, or adding dynamic animations to the text and the different world elements.

  • Game Designer & Programmer
    For every dream sequence there is a unique mini-game that mirrors the narrative, each one based on simple clicking and dragging interactions. I was responsible for implementing the game mechanics, game feel, in-engine animations, and sound mixing.

Facts & Accolades
  • Developed for the course Critical Making, from the Master of Arts (MA) in Game Design at Uppsala Universitet - Campus Gotland. The given challenge was to "develop a game which critically explores any idea, concept, or question related to gender". Our goal was to make a light-hearted toned celebration of girlhood and femininity through reparative game design, and a story that displays mostly non-normative personalities in girls and women.

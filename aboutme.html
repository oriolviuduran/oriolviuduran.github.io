About Me
Personally
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I'm a Junior Game Designer. I describe myself as creative, analytical, detail-oriented, easily adaptable and a team player. My main hobbies are playing story-rich video games, running, collecting vinyls and doing geography quizzes.

Professionally
I'm currently looking to join a game studio and begin my professional career as a game designer. I enjoy working on small narrative-driven solo projects under the name North Oriole Games with the intention of expressing myself and grow both as a game developer and as a human being.

Academically
On 2022 I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games from the Universitat de Girona. My Final Degree Project, which consisted in designing and developing the prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks and the "Computer Field Award" at the 28th Edition of the Premis Patronat. A year later I attended the course in Narrative Design for Video Games from Pulse College to become a better interactive storyteller.

About Me
A little backstory
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I'm a Game Designer and Developer. I was born in Girona in 1999 and, for as long as I can remember, I have always had a passion for creating stories, characters and worlds. Game-making is ideal for me, since it allows me to mix this creative passion along with my technological background.

Current doings
I have recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games at the University of Girona (UdG). My End of Degree Project was awarded full marks and consisted in designing and developing a prototype for WAVA, a cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer about self-growth and finding your place.
I'm currently looking to join a game studio and begin my professional career. In my spare time, I also work on solo projects under the name North Oriole Games so as to keep learning, growing and improving my skills.

Self-portrait
I consider myself to be an organised, communicative, versatile and creative person, with a high capacity for critical thinking, teamwork and always very eager to keep learning and growing. My main hobbies include playing video games, running, collecting vinyls and learning anything and everything geography-related.

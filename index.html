Hi there!

My name is Oriol and I’m an aspiring Game Designer, currently pursuing a Master’s in Sweden. I have experience both working solo under the name North Oriole, and from a diverse bunch of team projects made during my studies and game jams - though I'm usually drawn to experimental and minimalistic approaches.

I have a special love for games; some of my fondest memories are watching my older sister play The Sims, the long summers in front of my Wii, or bringing my PSP with me everywhere. Now, making games allows me to combine my technical background with a desire for meaningful interactive storytelling.

I’m creative, analytical, detail-oriented and versatile - always communicative and open to help, but also great at working independently. I'm also an avid runner, filmgoer, record collector, trivia nerd, and Simpsons enthusiast!

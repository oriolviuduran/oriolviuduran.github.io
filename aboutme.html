About Me
Personally
My name is Oriol Viu Duran and I'm a Junior Game Designer. I would describe myself as creative, analytical, detail-oriented and an easily adaptable team player. Also a story enjoyer, cozy gamer, runner, vinyl collector and a bit of a geography buff!

Professionally
I'm currently looking to join a team/studio and begin my career either around Barcelona or abroad. I develop small narrative-driven games in solitary under the name North Oriole with the intention of expressing myself and becoming a better game maker. My most notable shipped project is WAVA, a short cinematic 2D puzzle-platformer about self-growth and finding your place. I also like to participate in game jams and other team projects that allow me to keep learning and growing. Our entry for the Indie Spain Jam 2023, a platformer about mushrooms and teamwork titled Very Mush Alike, was chosen as one of the 25 finalists.

Academically
On 2022 I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games from the Universitat de Girona. My Final Degree Project, which consisted in designing and developing the prototype for WAVA, was awarded full marks and the "Computer Field Award" at the 28th Edition of the Premis Patronat. A year later I attended the course in Narrative Design for Video Games from Pulse College to improve my writing and interactive storytelling skills.
